    • In the Beginning . . .

      Metaphysics is said to be the study of "being-qua-being". That is to say, the nature of the study of metaphysics studies the nature of things insofar as it exists. The fundamental axioms of metaphysics are eixtence, consciousness, and identity.

      20 minutes

    • The Primacy of Existence vs. The Primacy of Consciousness

      The basic metaphysical issue that lies at the root of any system of philosophy is the primacy of existence or the primacy of consciousness. The primacy of existence states that existence exists and has primacy over consciousness. The primacy of consciousness states that mere thoughts shape reality and existence is at the whims of a mind.

      25 minutes

    • Entities, Causality, & Action

      The more particular subjects of metaphysics can be directly derived from the existence axiom. Among these more particular subjects are entities, attributes of entities, causality, and actions.

      28 minutes

    • The Supernatural as a Metaphysical Impossibility

      The arguments for the existence of the supernatural deny the very axioms they depend on. These contradictions show a dire fallacy in the concept of a supernatural entity, further, the arguments for a supernatural entity posit an erroneous philosophy riddled with improper premises which are snipped from its root: the primacy of consciousness.

      17 minutes
